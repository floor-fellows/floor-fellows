Our Services

Floors set the tone for every space — and keeping them looking their best takes skill. That's why Floor Fellows offers expert care for homes and businesses alike. Our team combines proven techniques with personalized service, ensuring your floors don't just look clean, but truly impress.


  • Deep Cleaning

    • Our deep cleaning service removes dirt, grime, and buildup that regular mopping can't touch. Using professional-grade equipment and solutions designed for hard floors, we restore your surfaces to a clean, fresh appearance without damaging their finish — leaving you with a healthier, brighter space you'll notice the moment you walk in.


  • Stripping & Waxing

    • Stripping and waxing can be messy, time-consuming, and labor-intensive work. At Floor Fellows, we handle the process quickly and efficiently, removing years of buildup and applying a fresh, protective finish while maintaining the high standard of quality our customers expect.


  • Sealing & Polishing

    • Natural stone floors are an investment, and protecting them is essential. Our sealing and polishing process strengthens the surface against stains, scratches, and daily wear while enhancing the stone's natural character. The result is a rich finish that highlights the beauty of your floors and helps them last for years to come.


  • Concrete Polishing

    • Polished concrete transforms ordinary slabs into stylish, versatile floors, all without the harsh chemicals. Whether you prefer a soft, understated sheen or a striking, high-gloss finish, the choice is yours. Beyond its beauty, polished concrete is one of the easiest surfaces to clean and maintain, making it ideal for both homes and businesses.
